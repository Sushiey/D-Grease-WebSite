About D-Grease

D-Grease (UK) Limited is an independent supplier of degreasing machines who have been operating now for over 20 years.

The value of our business is simple. A wide range of superior de-greasing equipment combined with a high standard of customer service along with professionalism at competitive prices.

Fully Compliant & Environmentally Committed

The correct handling and disposal of special waste has never been more essential in business not to mention the importance of protecting the environment.


The ever-widening scope of legislation, both current and in the future, along with an increasing trend towards litigation requires the need for the most professional approach to special waste of paramount importance.
